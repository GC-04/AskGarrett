About the Company

I had the pleasure of working with AZ Grass Team at Fastsigns. They were in need of a logo and vehicle graphics. I created a quick style guide to them with some mock ups. They could only give me a little input on their want for a mascot "three cacti mowing a lawn". I was unable to sell them on apparel, but was successful in selling them on a logo and mascot with our spectacular sales team and get them the vehicle graphic they wanted. Grass Team LLC is renowned for delivering top-notch workmanship and high-quality products in the installation of various landscaping features such as artificial turf, pavers, curbing, irrigation, and landscape lighting, among others.
